LPU Admit - admission details & application.

LPU Admit is an online portal that enables students to kick-start their enriching journey at LPU where one can know more about the programme of their interest and proceed with admission. They can also apply for LPUNEST for B.Tech Admission, check their result, download offer & acceptance letter (international Applicants), avail residential and other facilities.

LPUNEST 2021: The LPUNEST is an entrance exam conducted by the Lovely Professional University for admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate courses. LPUNEST 2021 is expected to be conducted as per schedule for admission to programs like B.Tech, Integrated B.Tech and MBA/M. Tech, LLM and PhD. LPU NEST score serves as the eligibility for admission to such courses offered by the University. On the other hand, candidates can optionally appear in the LPUNEST 2021 exam for availing scholarship for various programs. All the latest updates of LPUNEST 2021 can be checked from the official website of the University i.e. lpu.in and nest.lpu.in. Check here complete details of the LPUNEST 2021 exam including important dates, eligibility criteria, courses offered and other aspects.
